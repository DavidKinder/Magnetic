Documentation from the Original Packages

To prevent software pirates from spreading their programs, Magnetic Scrolls added password protection to most of their games. However, the extraction programs will offer to remove this type of protection. Despite this, most games still cannot be won without the original documentation, since it often contains vital clues. Stefan Meier's Magnetic Scrolls Memorial web page offers a growing amount of scanned material from the original packages. Further material is also available from the Magnetic Scrolls Gallery.
